Biggest and best AC Marriage / Wedding Hall in Palakkad District

THEVARCAD CONVENTION CENTRE is situated at Valliyode, Vadakencherry, Palakkad District. It has two fully airconditioned hall - A large hall which can seat 1000 persons and a Mini Hall for 500 persons. Parking is not a problem here with ample car parking space. On the first floor there are 7 large bed rooms for overnight stay or for privacy while dressing the bride and other guests.

The Thevarcad Convention centre is without doubt the largest and most convenient auditorium in Palakkad district. It is conveniently located near the main Thrissur Palakkad Road in Vadakkencherry, Palakkad District

Special Features of Thevarcad Convention Centre & Mariage Hall

  1. Fully Airconditioned
  2. Auditorium hall with 1000 seats and big stage
  3. Separate Dining Hall can seat upto 550
  4. Smaller Conference Hall with 500 seats
  5. 7 Spacious airconditioned bedrooms upstairs of the hall
  6. 100% Generator Backup, so no fear of Electric failure
  7. Very big area for car parking
